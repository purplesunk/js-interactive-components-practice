Mage

+

The arcane magic available to magi is both great and dangerous. To avoid interference with spellcasting, magi wear only cloth armor, but arcane shields and enchantments give them additional protection.

Warrior

+

Warriors combine strength, leadership, and a vast knowledge of arms and armor to wreak havoc in glorious combat.

Hunter

+

Masters of their environment, they are able to slip like ghosts through the trees and lay traps in the paths of their enemies.

Paladin

+

These holy warriors are equipped with plate armor so they can confront the toughest of foes, and the blessing of the Light allows them to heal wounds and, in some cases, even restore life to the dead.
